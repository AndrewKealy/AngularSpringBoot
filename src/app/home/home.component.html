Welcome to Golf Stars!

This is a game where you can pit your knowledge about the game of golf against friends and strangers and see who comes out on top

The game is straightforward.

1) Create a new group or join and existing one

2) Each group has one, two, four or six members

3) Pick your preferred golfers in the current tournament. The size of the group dictates how many picks you get

In a group of one or two players, each player gets to choose six golfers. In a group of four, each player gets four choices.

In a group of six, each player can choose three golfers

4) Once everyone has made their selections (on a first come / first served basis), points will be awarded based on tournament results

5) A first place player earns 10 points, second place eight points, third to sixth place earns six points, seventh to twelfth earns four points and thirteenth to twentieth earns two points

Have fun and good luck!
